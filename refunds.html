Returns & Refunds

Effective Date: June 5, 2026

If your minimal apparel capsule does not fit or coordinate as expected, we are happy to assist. We offer a simple return process within 30 days of shipment receipt.

1. Return Conditions

To qualify for a refund, items must be in their original condition:

  • Unworn, unwashed, and undamaged.
  • Original designer paper tags, brand stickers, and security tags must remain attached.
  • Housed in original protective cotton bag wrap.

2. Initiating a Return

To start a return, email your order details to returns@aurawear-store.com. We will provide a pre-paid shipping label. Print the label, secure it to the original package boxes, and drop off at any local courier center.

3. Refund Timescales

Once received and inspected by our warehouse quality team, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. If paid by online card, the funds will return to the original bank method. If paid by Cash on Delivery (COD), we will contact you to coordinate a bank wire transfer or store credit card discount.

4. Exchange Requests

We handle exchanges by returning your initial order and placing a new one. This ensures automatic tracking of item variants, inventories, and GTM data layer checkout actions.
