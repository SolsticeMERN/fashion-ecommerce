Shipping Policy

Effective Date: June 5, 2026

We aim to pack, process, and ship your minimal wardrobe capsule with care and speed. Below you will find detailed rules regarding shipping rates and delivery schedules.

1. Processing Times

All orders are packed and shipped from our SoHo facility within 1-2 business days. Orders placed before 1:00 PM EST are shipped same-day (excluding weekends and corporate holidays).

2. Shipping Speeds & Rates

Shipping Method Duration Rate
Standard Delivery 3 - 5 business days $15.00 (Free for orders over $150)
Express Courier 1 - 2 business days $25.00

3. Free Shipping Threshold

Orders that have a subtotal (after discounts and coupons) equal to or exceeding $150.00 receive complimentary standard shipping. You can also apply specific coupon codes (such as SAVESHIP) to waive standard fees.

4. Order Inspection & Cash on Delivery

For Cash on Delivery shipments, the courier is instructed to allow package inspection before taking payment. Please ensure you have the exact cash ready when delivery is scheduled.
