Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 5, 2026

At Aura Wear, we take the confidentiality and security of our clients' personal details seriously. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, process, and protect your information when you browse our site, make purchases, or sign up for our communication listings.

1. Data Collection and Usage

We collect personal identifiers when explicitly provided by you (such as your email address, phone number, name, shipping details) during checkout, registrations, or form inquiries. This information is utilized solely to process purchases, fulfill deliveries, and send coupon codes.

2. Analytical Tracking and Cookies

To optimize our catalog layouts, measure marketing campaigns, and improve loading speed, we use industry-standard tracking technologies including Google Tag Manager (GTM), Google Analytics 4 (GA4), and Meta Pixel. These scripts set temporary cookies (like _fbp and _fbc) and collect technical variables:

  • Client ID (unique device browser cookie identifier)
  • Session ID (tracks browsing duration)
  • UTM Campaign Referrers (UTM source, medium, campaign)
  • Google Click ID (gclid) and Meta Click ID (fbclid)

3. Server-Side Hashing & Protection

To safeguard customer PII (Personally Identifiable Information) during marketing integration, we use one-way SHA-256 cryptographic hashing algorithms prior to transmission to servers (such as Meta Conversions API or Google Enhanced Conversions). This ensures your email or telephone number is never transmitted as plain text.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to opt-out of cookie tracking at any time by configuring your browser settings or deleting cookies. If you wish to request deletion of your registered account or billing details, please contact care@aurawear-store.com.
