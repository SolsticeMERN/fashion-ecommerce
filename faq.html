Frequently Asked Questions

What materials do you use in your products? ▾
We use 100% organic cotton, extra-fine merino wool, premium mulberry silk, and natural flax linen. We avoid synthetic blends (like polyester or nylon) to ensure maximum breathability, natural drape, and environmental responsibility.
How do I track my order? ▾
Once your package is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via email. You can also view active order statuses directly inside your Customer Dashboard under the "Order History" tab.
Do you support Cash on Delivery (COD)? ▾
Yes! We support Cash on Delivery (COD) for all retail shipments, allowing you to pay in cash only after receiving and inspecting the package contents.
What is your return policy? ▾
We offer a complimentary 30-day return policy for all unworn garments with original tags attached. Please visit our Returns & Refunds page or contact care@aurawear-store.com to request a shipping label.
Do you ship internationally? ▾
Currently, we ship to the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom. Standard international shipping fees are calculated during checkout.
