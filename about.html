Our Heritage

Minimalism Reimagined

Designed for longevity. Spun for luxury.

Founded in 2024, Aura Wear was born from a desire to break away from the cycle of disposable fashion. We believe that true luxury lies in restraint — in garments that do not shout, but convey elegance through structural form, silhouette, and the purity of their fibers.

Every item in our collection is designed in-house, balancing classical tailoring with modern utility lines. We source our organic cotton, fine flax linen, and heavy wool from family-owned heritage mills in Italy, Peru, and Japan.

Responsible Fiber

We use 100% natural, biodegradable yarns and organic dyes, keeping microplastics out of ecosystems.

Exceptional Craft

Our supplier factories guarantee fair wages, safe environments, and limit water waste during production.

Capsule Focus

We manufacture limited batches, reducing inventory waste and ensuring unmatched attention to detail.
